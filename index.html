Operation

Onin Textile Mills is a truly integrated undertaking. The goal of the Textile division is to become the preferred partner for sourcing high quality fabrics and clothing from Bangladesh. With highly advanced technology and an emphasis on developing local human resources, the Textile Division has the potential to make an important contribution to the nation’s growing fabrics manufacturing export sector.

Future Plans

In order to accelerate our present strong background linkage base, we are actively thinking establishment to expansion of different dimension of backward linkage up to RMG industries for integration as part of value addition. We are going to establishment a fashion house also.

Onin Textile Mills is 100% Export Oriented Fabrics Manufacturer & Producing High Quality Woven Fabrics

Onin Textile Mills is the most modern weaving mills in Narsingdi of Bangladesh. It has an installed capacity of 20 high-speed rapier looms (Air Jet 10 looms, Tsudokoma 10 looms, Saurer S 400 10 sets), 90 Nos medium speed looms as described below. Onin Textile Mills have been producing about 27,000 yards of grey woven fabrics per day ranging from 80GSM to 310GSM of the medium Twill, Poplin, T/C, T/R, Cotton Sheeting, Herringbone, and all kinds of pocketing fabrics etc. to meet the standards of the most discerning international buyers.

Child Labor

                    
Onin Textile Mills does not employ any person below the age of 18 years to produce apparel, accessories or equipment.

                    
Wages & Salary

                    
Onin Textile Mills provides each employee at least minimum wage or higher and provides each employee a clear,Written accounting for every pay period.

                    
Benefits

                    
Onin Textile Mills provides each employee with legally mandated benefits. These include health care, pregnancy or sickvacation, religious holiday, leave, child care facilities.
